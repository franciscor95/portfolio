Indie Lisboa 2020

/

Client: Academic

/

Category: Branding & Identity

Brand identity designed for the 2020 edition of Indie Lisboa as part of an academic project. Indie Lisboa is an alternative film festival happening annualy in the Portuguese capital. Each year their visual communication strategy changes, keeping only their logo constant. This project aimed to create a fresh visual identity for the 2020 edition, starting from the creation of a new logo. This new identity was strongly focused on the close relationship of the festival with its host city Lisbon, which translated into the use of the crow, an iconic symbol of the city, and other famous landmarks.

Belém Posters

/

Client: Acedemic

/

Category: Posters

Posters designed for Belém, an historical portuguese neighbourhood, strongly influenced by the portuguese discoveries. This academic project was inspired by the work of portuguese artist Vasco Araújo, where he reflected about the european vision of colonialism and how that translates into today's society. His work is focused on the staues representing african indegenous people that are still present today in Belém's Botanical Garden, alongside monkeys and other african animal species. I used these same statues on the posters.

Lisboa Verde

/

Client: Academic

/

Category: Editorial & Video

Lisboa Verde: The Essential Guide for Well-Being in Lisbon's Gardens is an editorial project inspired by Lisbon's being the european green capital for 2020. The guide covers some of the city's most iconic gardens while proposing activities aiming to improve mental health and well-being. It also comes with a promotional video, a collection of four posters, postcards and a map showing the location of these gardens in the city.
